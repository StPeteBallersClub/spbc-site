More Than Volleyball

SPBC is a community-first beach volleyball club built on one idea: create the most welcoming, highest-energy place to play in St. Pete — for everyone, from first-timers to seasoned competitors.

We run free weekly pickup across St. Pete's best beaches, plus competitive leagues and tournaments that consistently sell out. Live DJs, officiated courts, real signage, and a culture people genuinely love.

Volleyball brings us together. The connections keep us here.

Play

Free weekly pickup at North Shore, Woodlawn, and St. Pete Beach. Show up, get reps, meet your people.

Compete

Tournaments and leagues with officiated courts, real divisions, and an experience nobody else in St. Pete delivers.

Belong

A tight, trusting community that shows up week after week — and looks out for each other on and off the sand.
