Why Now

SPBC has been founder-funded from day one. The free pickup, the officiated tournaments, the signage and the energy — all of it has been built and paid for to keep this community thriving.

The Founders Club is how we make SPBC sustainable without ever putting a price on the thing that matters most: showing up and playing. Members fund the future. Everyone keeps the culture.

The Free Stuff Stays Free

Weekly pickup, the WhatsApp community, and the welcoming, all-levels culture are non-negotiable and always free.

Membership sits on top of all of that — it's for the people who want more and want to help build what's next. Nobody loses access by not being a member.
