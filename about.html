Our Story

Built On
The Sand

SPBC started as informal pickup volleyball and grew into St. Pete's fastest-growing beach volleyball community — built around people first, competition second, and good energy always.

Who We Are

St. Pete Ballers Club is a community-first beach volleyball club with one mission: create the most welcoming, highest-energy place to play in St. Pete — for every skill level and every background.

We host free weekly pickup across St. Pete's best beaches, plus competitive leagues and tournaments that consistently sell out. Live DJs, officiated courts, real signage, and a culture people genuinely love.

We're more than volleyball. The game brings people together — the connections are what keep them here.

What We Stand For

  • Inclusivity — all skill levels, all backgrounds, always welcome.
  • Energy — music, movement, and a high-vibe environment every session.
  • Community first — people before everything else.
  • Competitive excellence — serious volleyball without the exclusivity.
  • Show up for each other — on the sand and off it.

How To Join

1

Reach Out

Fill out the form below or DM us "JOIN" on Instagram. We'll point you to the next session.

2

3

Pull up to any free pickup session. No experience necessary — just bring your energy.

Drop your info and we'll get you connected to the community, the schedule, and your first session. It's free to start playing — pickup always is.
